# Example Core Tasks — TaskFlow

## Task 1: See what's due today
Right after opening the app, figure out what you need to do today and in what
order.

## Task 2: Add a repeating task
Add a new task that repeats weekly, due at a specific time, and confirm it
shows up correctly.

## Task 3: Mark a habit done for today
Find today's habit tracker and mark "Drink water" as done for today.

## Task 4: Export your data
Get a clean copy of all your tasks out of the app (for backup or to move to
another tool).
